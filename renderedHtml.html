Who are we?

We are the University of Florida\'s student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery. We are an organization dedicated to the promotion of computer science research, education, and involvement within the students of the CISE department.

What do we do?

During the year, you can find us bringing companies like Google and Microsoft to campus, promoting cool Special Interest Groups, and throwing awesome department-wide events. You should checkout some of our Special Interest Groups (SIGs) in the top right corner!

Where are we?

Generally we are all over the place with our meetings and info-sessions, but our main office, the Fishbowl is located across the pathway in front of Marston Science library\'s entrance. Our door has the large, blue ACM logo on it. It\'s on the right side of the photo below!
